Battery Memo Support

Battery Memo is a simple iPhone app that records which batteries your remotes, thermometers, clocks, toys, flashlights, and other household items use.

FAQ

Q. Where is my data stored?
All data is stored only on your device. No login, no ads, and nothing is sent to external servers.

Q. What happens to my data if I delete the app?
Deleting the app also deletes its data. If you use iCloud Backup, the data may be included in your device backup.

Q. How do I delete a device or battery I registered?
Press and hold a card in the list to delete it.
