Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") describes how information is handled in the app "Battery Memo" (the "App"). The developer of the App (the "Developer") respects your privacy and handles information as follows.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

Device names, photos, battery types, stock counts, storage locations, and notes you register in the App are stored only on your device. The Developer cannot access or view them.

2. External Transmission and Third Parties

The App does not send your data to any external server and does not share it with any third party.

The App also uses none of the following:

  • Login features (account registration)
  • Advertising
  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking of user behavior

3. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App accesses the camera and photo library only to let you add photos of your devices and battery storage locations.

Access is requested through the standard iOS permission system. Photos you take or select are stored only on your device and are never transmitted externally.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

All registered data is stored on your device, and deleting the App also deletes its data.

If you use iOS backup features such as iCloud Backup, the App's data may be included in your device backup. Backups are governed by Apple's privacy policy and your device settings.

5. Changes to This Policy

The Developer may revise this Policy in response to legal changes or updates to the App's features. Material changes will be announced within the App.
